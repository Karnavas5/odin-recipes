Spaghetti Sandwhich

Description

One time, my mom told me to make a spaghetti sandwhich. So I did. Here's how.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Two Slices of Bread
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Take two slices of bread and butter one side of each.
  2. With the butter side facing up, place your desired amount of spaghetti on one slice of bread.
  3. Take the other slice and put it on top of the spaghetti
  4. That's it. Spaghetti Sandwhich.
